A welcome

You're welcome here

Imam Sherif keeps his door open to every member of the community — whether you're navigating marriage, raising a family, new to Islam, or simply have something on your heart.

Get to know the imam

A life of service

Imam Sherif has a passion for helping and uplifting others — in moments of celebration, in times of grief, and in the quiet questions in between.

For over twenty years he has been a religious leader to the Muslim communities of Fredericksburg, Stafford, and Culpeper, and has served as the imam of Masjid Aliya since 2010 — leading prayer and the Friday khutbah, performing weddings and funerals, and walking with families through marriage, difficulty, and everything in between.

His path bridges law and faith: he earned a degree in law from Ain Shams University and studied Hadith at Al-Azhar University, with diplomatic studies at the University of Vienna. He speaks English, Arabic, and German.

Beyond the masjid, he serves as a chaplain for Stafford Fire & Rescue and the Stafford County Sheriff’s Office, and gives back as Vice President of the Stafford Diversity Advisory Coalition and a member of the county’s Faith Leaders Advisory Committee.
