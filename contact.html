Frequently Asked

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What's the best way to reach Imam Sherif quickly?
Calling (540) 842-5840 is the fastest. Email is also monitored and responses typically arrive within one to two business days.
Can I just stop by without an appointment?
Walk-ins are welcome when the Imam is available, but we strongly recommend booking online or calling ahead to secure your time.
I'm not Muslim — is it okay to reach out?
Absolutely. Imam Sherif serves the entire Stafford community, Muslim and non-Muslim alike. All are welcome.
